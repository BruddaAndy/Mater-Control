# Mater-Control
This is my individual modeling for the master control project.
Lead Conversion Analysis & Modeling
Business Problem

This project examines why one product line (Mx) has lower lead conversion rates than another (Qx), and how to better identify leads that are likely to convert.

Improving conversion helps sales teams focus on the right opportunities and increases overall revenue potential.

Solution

I analyzed the data to understand differences in conversion across segments and built a model to predict whether a lead will progress through the sales funnel.

The approach included:

Cleaning and preparing the data
Defining a “progressed” outcome
Comparing performance across product lines
Building a Random Forest model
Evaluating performance using ROC/AUC

My Contributions
Cleaned and prepared the dataset in R
Created the target variable for lead progression
Performed exploratory analysis to identify patterns
Built and evaluated a Random Forest model
Developed visualizations and interpreted results
Business Value

This model helps identify high-quality leads earlier, allowing teams to prioritize outreach and improve efficiency.

This can lead to:

Better targeting of promising leads
More efficient use of sales and marketing resources
Higher conversion rates
Challenges
Handling missing and inconsistent data
Selecting meaningful features
Avoiding overfitting
Translating technical results into business insights
